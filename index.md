
# Privacy Policy

KannadaBuddy ("we", "our", "the app") respects your privacy.

**Information we collect**
• Text and images you provide (Kannada input) are sent to our servers only to perform OCR, transliteration, and translation. We do not store this content after processing.
• Usage data: free-attempt count and subscription status are stored locally on your device (and, if you subscribe, with the app store).

**How we use information**
• Your Kannada text and images are used solely to return transliteration and English meaning. They are not used for marketing or shared with third parties.
• We use the same backend and translation services only to fulfil your request.

**Data retention**
• We do not retain your input text or images on our servers after the response is sent.
• Local app data (e.g. subscription flag) remains on your device until you clear app data or uninstall.

**Your rights**
• You can stop using the app at any time and clear app data to remove local information.
• For subscription or account questions, contact us at kannadabuddya@gmail.com.

**Changes**
• We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
